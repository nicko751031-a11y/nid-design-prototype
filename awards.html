水里蛇窯 Shuili Snake Kiln

1920年代歷史窯場轉化為現代藝廊與零售空間，體現 Adaptive Reuse 設計理念，是川果設計國際聲譽的核心作品。 A 1920s heritage kiln site transformed into a contemporary gallery and retail space, embodying the Adaptive Reuse design philosophy. This is the cornerstone project of NID Design Lab's international reputation.
