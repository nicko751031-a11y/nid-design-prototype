What We Do

服務項目 Services Services

01

住宅空間設計 Residential Design

Residential Design

以居住者的生活方式為核心，打造兼具美感與機能的理想居家空間。從格局規劃、動線設計到材質選配，每一個細節都為生活品質而生。我們相信，家不只是一個空間，更是生活態度的延伸。 Crafting ideal living spaces that balance aesthetics and functionality, centered around each resident's lifestyle. From layout planning and circulation design to material selection, every detail serves the quality of life. We believe home is not just a space, but an extension of one's attitude toward living.

02

商業空間設計 Commercial Design

Commercial Design

為品牌創造獨特的空間體驗，涵蓋餐飲、零售、辦公與文化場域。我們深入理解品牌核心價值，將其轉化為可感知的空間語言，讓每一位訪客都能感受到品牌的故事與溫度。 Creating unique spatial experiences for brands across dining, retail, office, and cultural venues. We deeply understand each brand's core values, translating them into tangible spatial language, allowing every visitor to feel the brand's story and warmth.

03

建築外觀設計 Architectural Design

Architectural Design

從建築立面到室內空間的整合設計，業界少見的雙軌設計服務。我們的建築外觀設計不僅關注視覺美感，更注重與周圍環境的對話、材質的耐候性，以及建築與室內的一體化設計思維。 Integrated design from facade to interior — a rare dual-track service in the industry. Our architectural design focuses not only on visual aesthetics but also on dialogue with the surrounding environment, material durability, and a unified design philosophy connecting exterior and interior.

04

全屋訂製 Full Home Customization

Full Home Customization

系統櫃體與訂製家具的一站式整合服務。由設計師主導的全屋訂製，確保每一個收納單元都與空間設計完美契合。2026年全新品牌「川川國際」，以設計師的品質標準，提供更靈活的訂製方案。 One-stop custom cabinetry and bespoke furniture integration. Designer-led full home customization ensures every storage unit perfectly harmonizes with the spatial design. The all-new 2026 brand "Chuanchuan International" delivers flexible custom solutions at designer-quality standards.
