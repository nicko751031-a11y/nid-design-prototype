Our Approach

設計理念Design Philosophy

侘寂美學Wabi-sabi Aesthetics

擁抱不完美之美，在時間的痕跡中找到寧靜與深度，讓空間散發自然而然的質感。Embracing the beauty of imperfection, finding tranquility and depth in the traces of time, letting spaces radiate an effortless texture.

光影運用Light & Shadow

策略性引入自然光，讓光影成為空間的第二建材，隨時間推移創造流動的空間層次。Strategically introducing natural light, making light and shadow the second building material that creates fluid spatial layers as time passes.

材質本真Material Authenticity

尊重每一種材料的原始語言，展現木、石、金屬、混凝土的本質美感，而非裝飾性覆蓋。Respecting the original language of every material — revealing the inherent beauty of wood, stone, metal, and concrete rather than decorative concealment.

歷史保存Adaptive Reuse

以 Adaptive Reuse 的理念，為歷史空間注入當代生命力，在保存與創新之間找到平衡。Breathing contemporary life into heritage spaces through Adaptive Reuse, finding the balance between preservation and innovation.

減法設計Subtractive Design

去除一切不必要的元素，讓空間回歸本質。少，即是多。留白，即是豐盈。Removing all unnecessary elements to return space to its essence. Less is more. Emptiness is abundance.

空間敘事Spatial Narrative

每一個空間都有它的故事。我們透過設計語彙，為場域譜寫屬於它的獨特篇章。Every space has its own story. Through our design vocabulary, we compose a unique chapter for each venue.
